üß† Prompt Logic & Strategy
I utilized a Role-Context-Goal framework to ensure the AI output remained professional and industry-specific:

Role Prompting: Established the AI as a "Senior Conversion Copywriter" to prioritize clarity and sales over fluff.

Context Injection: Provided specific details about the "Quiet Zone" and "In-house roasting" to avoid generic "coffee shop" text.

Constraint Engineering: Banned common "AI-isms" (e.g., tapestry, delve, unlock) to ensure a human-centric, local brand voice.

Benefit-Driven Messaging: Focused on the result (productivity and focus) rather than just the product (caffeine).
